### CHANNEL 1: UNDERWRITING — Target $150,000
**Owner: Terrence Ballard** | **Closer: Louise**

**Current state:** Roughly $60,000/year from a handful of local underwriters.
**Gap to close:** $90,000 — find and sign 8–12 new underwriters.

**Step-by-step plan:**

1. **Audit existing underwriters** (Month 1) — Who's current? Who's lapsed? Who's a renewal opportunity?
2. **Build the prospect list** (Month 1–2) — 50 Lorain/Northeast Ohio businesses that serve the WNZN Power audience. Target: healthcare, funeral homes, auto dealers, local banks, churches with businesses, legal services.
3. **Set the underwriting rate card** (Month 1) — 3 tiers: Community ($3,000/year), Supporter ($7,500/year), Premier ($15,000/year). All FCC-compliant acknowledgment scripts included.
4. **Launch outreach** (Month 2) — Terrence works the list. Louise handles follow-up and closing. Goal: 2 new signed underwriters per month.
5. **Renewal automation** (Month 3) — Jessie builds a Make.com workflow: 90-day, 60-day, 30-day renewal reminders. No underwriter falls off without a personal call.
6. **Regional expansion** (Month 6) — After local market is seeded, expand to Cleveland metro businesses that want to reach the gospel/Black church community in Northeast Ohio.

**FCC COMPLIANCE NOTE:** All on-air acknowledgments must be factual, no calls to action, no price mentions, no qualitative superlatives. Terrence reviews every script. Bishop Hank audits quarterly.

**Monthly milestone:** 2 new underwriters signed per month = 24 new over 12 months. Average $4,000/year each = +$96,000. Plus renewals and upgrades = $150,000 total.

### CHANNEL 2: MAJOR DONORS — Target $50,000
**Owner: Moriah Ballard** | **Closer: Louise**

**Current state:** Minimal formal major donor program. Most giving is transactional or event-based.
**Phase 1 goal:** Identify and cultivate 10 major donor prospects. Close 5 at an average of $10,000 each.

**Step-by-step plan:**

1. **Build the prospect list** (Month 1) — 25 individuals who: (a) attend Black churches in Lorain/Cleveland, (b) have giving capacity of $5K+, (c) already believe in gospel music and community media. Start with people Dr. Ballard already knows.
2. **Create the donor story** (Month 2) — Barbara/Nana writes a one-page impact document. "Here's what WNZN Power means to Lorain, Ohio. Here's what your gift makes possible." No grant language. Real talk.
3. **One-on-one cultivation** (Month 2–6) — Dr. Ballard personally meets with top 10 prospects. Coffee, lunch, a station tour. No ask in the first meeting. Just relationship.
4. **The Ask** (Month 4–8) — After cultivation, Moriah sends the formal ask letter. Louise follows up. Dr. Ballard calls the ones who need to hear his voice.
5. **Donor stewardship system** (Month 3, ongoing) — Every donor gets: thank-you call within 48 hours, quarterly impact update, personal Christmas card, name recognition on air (FCC-compliant).
6. **End-of-year giving push** (November–December) — Moriah runs a year-end campaign. Email sequence through Mailchimp. Goal: 3 additional gifts before December 31.

**Year 1 reality check:** $50,000 is conservative. If Dr. Ballard personally engages 10 people and 5 give, this is achievable. The real payoff is Year 2 and 3 when those 5 give again and refer others.

## PHASE 2: SCALE
**Months 13–24 | Target: $600,000 | July 2027 – June 2028**

### What changes in Phase 2
- Underwriting: Move from local-only to regional. Add a national gospel advertising category.
- Donors: Launch a formal major gifts program. Target 5 donors at $25,000+.
- Events: Add a second major event. Explore a gala format for major donors.
- Grants: Pursue larger grants ($25,000–$50,000). CPB (Corporation for Public Broadcasting) eligibility research.

### Phase 2 Key Moves
1. **Hire a part-time development coordinator** — Moriah needs human support by Month 14–15. This is the first hire the revenue justifies.
2. **Launch WNZN Power Listener Club** — Recurring monthly giving. Even $10/month from 200 listeners = $24,000/year of reliable revenue.
3. **Corporate underwriting push** — Greg identifies 3–5 regional corporate partners. Larger deals, multi-year commitments.
4. **Events become a brand** — Spring Gospel Jam Year 2 is bigger, better documented, and starts attracting national gospel acts.
