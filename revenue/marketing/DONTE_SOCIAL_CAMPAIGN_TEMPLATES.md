## VOICE RULES (from Barbara "Nana" Ballard)
*Every post goes through this filter before it goes live.*

- Say **"WNZN Power"** — never just "WNZN"
- Ground everything in **"Lorain, Ohio"** — specificity matters
- Warm, direct, community-first tone — never corporate
- Faith-aware but never preachy
- Short sentences. Real talk. No hashtag stuffing.
- Maximum 5 hashtags per post (Instagram); 2–3 on Facebook
- Dr. Ballard speaks in first person when posts are from him personally
- Station posts speak as "we" or "WNZN Power"

## DONTE'S MONTHLY CONTENT CALENDAR PROCESS

1. **First Monday of each month:** Donte drafts the full month's social calendar in Notion. Includes copy, visual direction, and any campaign activations.
2. **Barbara/Nana reviews** all copy for voice — approves or redlines.
3. **Donte finalizes and schedules** in the social scheduling tool.
4. **Live monitoring:** Donte checks comments and messages daily. Anything that needs a response within 24 hours. Escalate anything negative or sensitive to Dr. Ballard or Barbara/Nana before responding.
5. **End-of-month report to Seven Townsel:** reach, engagement, follower growth, link clicks.

---

## ESCALATION RULES FOR DONTE

- Any post mentioning faith, politics, race, or community controversy → Barbara/Nana reviews before posting.
- Any post from Dr. Ballard personally → Dr. Ballard approves the exact copy.
- Any post about the Lorain City Schools Board → run by Dr. Ballard and check Ohio Open Meetings Act implications.
- Any negative comment or public complaint → do not respond publicly without guidance. Flag to Barbara/Nana within 1 hour.
- Any underwriter or donor mentioned by name → Terrence or Moriah confirms approval first.
